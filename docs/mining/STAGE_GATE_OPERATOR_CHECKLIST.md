# Mining Stage Gate Operator Checklist

This checklist is the human-facing companion to `docs/mining/commercialization_gates.json`.

## Current status

Current stage: `stage_0_pre_validation_research_only`.

Commercial posture: research and validation in progress only.

Do not claim:

- guaranteed mining revenue;
- live pool-side hashrate;
- accepted-share performance;
- SHA-256 quantum acceleration;
- Antminer S21 or ASIC superiority;
- firmware licensing readiness;
- sovereign mining production readiness;
- SLA, warranty, or production field validation.

## Stage 1: structured double-SHA-256 validation

Before any structured-performance claim is made, require:

- real or testnet block-header fixture;
- actual double-SHA-256 nonce loop;
- classical enumeration baseline;
- phi-guided ordering baseline;
- at least 100 repeated runs with variance;
- written statement that the result is not pool-side hashrate or revenue evidence.

## Stage 2: firmware or hardware integration

Before firmware licensing or hardware-integration commercialization, require:

- Stage 1 complete;
- hardware or firmware adapter evidence;
- mixed-workload error handling;
- host power and hashrate measurement;
- bounded ASIC comparison with no replacement or superiority claim unless measured evidence supports it.

## Stage 3: field-trial production validation

Before SLA, warranty, pool-integration, sovereign mining, or production deployment claims, require:

- Stage 2 complete;
- at least six months of field-trial evidence;
- pool-side accepted-share telemetry;
- stale/reject/latency/power variance report;
- legal and compliance review.

## Required post-call written confirmation

After any external conversation about mining status, send and archive a written confirmation:

HYBA is validating the system and is not claiming guaranteed mining revenue, accepted-share performance, pool-side hashrate, SHA-256 quantum acceleration, ASIC superiority, firmware licensing readiness, or production sovereign mining readiness until the relevant evidence and stage gates pass.
