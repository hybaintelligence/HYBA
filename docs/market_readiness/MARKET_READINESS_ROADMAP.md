# Market Readiness Roadmap: Vectors G-L

Vectors G-L convert the validated HYBA platform into a launchable enterprise SaaS motion.

- **Vector G:** customer portal for usage, billing, API keys, workload history, and payment-method management.
- **Vector H:** multi-cloud Terraform and Helm deployment foundation for AWS, Azure, and GCP.
- **Vector I:** revenue analytics, ARR, churn risk, LTV/CAC, and Grafana reporting.
- **Vector J:** attorney-led patent/IP workflow.
- **Vector K:** substrate-independence adapters and benchmark harness.
- **Vector L:** SOC 2, customer success, partnerships, and marketplace GTM.

## Enterprise product standard

Market-readiness deliverables follow four operating principles expected by strategic buyers and hyperscale partners:

1. **Evidence-first telemetry:** customer-facing numbers must trace to operational systems or explicit fixture modes.
2. **Board-grade packaging:** portal language, workflows, and dashboards are suitable for CIO/CFO review.
3. **Hyperscale portability:** deployment artifacts keep cloud-specific concerns isolated while exposing one operator workflow.
4. **Partnership extensibility:** hardware adapters and GTM checklists are scaffolded for IBM, Google Cloud, Apple-class product-review expectations, and enterprise system integrators without overstating unverified runtime claims.
