# Patent and IP Strategy

This is an operational checklist for counsel, not legal advice.

## Prior-art search

- Search USPTO for quantum error-correction, oracle, and quantum-compute platform patents.
- Review Google Patents for competing claims and assignees.
- Review IEEE, ACM, arXiv, and vendor publications before claim drafting.

## Candidate claims for attorney review

1. Syndrome-derived decoder with separate correction telemetry and logical-error projections.
2. Φ-resonance oracle integration for structured nonce candidate search.
3. Multi-tenant quantum-compute platform with tenant isolation, quotas, dynamic code distance, and auditable claim boundaries.

## Filing timeline

- Week 1: retain patent counsel and prepare provisional disclosures.
- Weeks 2-3: complete attorney-led prior-art review and claim charting.
- Month 1: convert priority disclosures into utility filing plan.
- Months 3-6: evaluate PCT and international filings for target markets.
