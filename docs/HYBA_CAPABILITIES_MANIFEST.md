# HYBA Capabilities Manifest

## Positioning

HYBA should be presented as a safety-governed autonomous learning system. PYTHIA mining is the validation vehicle because it provides objective outcomes, fast feedback loops, and deterministic local verification. The broader platform pattern is: observe an objective response, update bounded evidence, propose a constrained adaptation, and preserve operator-grade auditability.

## Core Capability Stack

| Layer | Capability | Transferable Pattern |
| --- | --- | --- |
| METIS | Unified substrate for domain state, goals, and observations | Convert a domain into measurable state and objective feedback. |
| EUCLID | Emergent reasoning and counterfactual search | Generate candidate adaptations without requiring production mutation. |
| PYTHAGORAS | Hilbert-space and structural simulation primitives | Explore quantum-like/state-space structures on classical infrastructure. |
| PULVINI | Compression and retained-kernel memory | Keep learning state bounded while preserving operationally useful evidence. |
| COGITO | Safety governance, audit, and operator controls | Enforce proposal gates, fail-closed approval, and rollback discipline. |
| PYTHIA | Proof-of-work validation harness | Demonstrate objective feedback, deterministic replay, and live-response learning. |

## Mining Evidence as a Proof Vehicle

Mining is useful because every share has a binary pool outcome, every nonce can be locally checked, and the operational loop is easy to instrument. HYBA should avoid claiming guaranteed mining revenue or pool-side performance without real telemetry. The correct claim is that the system now has bounded autonomous-learning primitives that can be supervised, tested, and promoted through explicit gates.

## Transferable Domains

### Finance

- Replace pool accept/reject with trade or strategy outcome.
- Replace hashrate/latency with return, drawdown, slippage, and risk limits.
- Preserve fail-closed approvals for treasury, venue, and leverage changes.

### Supply Chain

- Replace nonce search with routing, inventory, or dispatch decisions.
- Replace pool response with delivery time, cost, spoilage, or service-level result.
- Preserve bounded history and deterministic replay for auditability.

### Biotech and Molecular Design

- Replace mining proposals with molecular geometry or candidate-screening proposals.
- Replace pool outcome with binding-energy, toxicity, assay, or simulation results.
- Preserve proposal-only safety posture until independently validated.

### Climate and Energy

- Replace mining efficiency with grid, load, storage, or renewable-balancing objectives.
- Replace response with measured energy, emissions, curtailment, or resilience outcomes.
- Preserve operator promotion gates for infrastructure-facing autonomy.

### Governance and Institutional Design

- Replace optimization target with policy configuration, workflow, or resource allocation.
- Replace response with measured compliance, throughput, fairness, or service outcome.
- Preserve claim-boundary review, transparency, and human override.

## Evidence Gates

HYBA's production story should remain tiered:

1. **Supervised**: operator approval required for guarded actions.
2. **Unattended**: requires sustained evidence pack, state recovery proof, and testnet/live validation.
3. **Autonomous**: requires unattended evidence plus chaos/game-day validation and rollback rehearsal.

## Narrative Discipline

Lead with: "HYBA runs safe autonomous learning loops over objective outcomes." Mining is the reference implementation, not the whole company story. Avoid unsupported claims about revenue, custody, regulatory compliance, or external performance until validated by the proper evidence path.
