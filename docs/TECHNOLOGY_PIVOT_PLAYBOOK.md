## Transition Plan: Mining → Platform

### **Mining Operations: Wind Down or Sustain?**

**Option A: Wind Down Mining** (Clean Break)

**Pros:**
- Full focus on platform business
- No Bitcoin price exposure
- Simpler narrative for investors

**Cons:**
- Lose recurring revenue stream
- Lose real-world validation testbed
- Lose case study credibility

**Recommendation:** ❌ **Not recommended in Year 1**

---

**Option B: Sustain Mining at Maintenance Level** (Hybrid)

**Approach:**
- Continue mining with **existing infrastructure** (no new ASIC investment)
- Use mining as **validation testbed** for new autonomous controller features
- Maintain mining as **reference customer** for platform demos

**Pros:**
- Recurring revenue funds R&D (~$500K-$1M/year)
- Real-world production environment for testing
- Credibility: "We use our own product in production"

**Cons:**
- Slight distraction (10-20% of eng time)
- Bitcoin price volatility (manageable with hedging)

**Recommendation:** ✅ **Recommended for Months 1-18**

**Transition:** Wind down mining after Series A (Month 18+) once SaaS revenue exceeds mining revenue.

---

**Option C: Spin Out Mining** (Strategic)

**Approach:**
- Create separate mining entity (MiningCo)
- License HYBA platform to MiningCo
- Founders retain equity in both entities

**Pros:**
- Clean separation for platform investors
- Mining continues generating cash
- Platform gets reference customer + license revenue

**Cons:**
- Complex cap table
- Operational overhead (2 entities)

**Recommendation:** ⏳ **Consider at Series A** (if mining still significant revenue)

## Risk Management

### **Pivot Risk: Loss of Focus**

**Risk:** Spreading too thin across mining + platform

**Mitigation:**
- **Strict time allocation:** 80% platform, 20% mining (max)
- **Mining as testbed only:** No new mining optimization features unless they generalize
- **Quarterly review:** If mining takes >25% of eng time, trigger wind-down decision

---

### **Pivot Risk: Customer Skepticism**

**Risk:** "You're a Bitcoin company. Why should we trust you with our infrastructure?"

**Mitigation:**
- **Reframe narrative:** "Bitcoin was the validation. This is the application."
- **Lead with technology:** Show autonomous controller, not mining
- **Use mining as proof:** "If it works for Bitcoin (adversarial, real-time, financial), it works for you"

---

### **Pivot Risk: Mining Revenue Cliff**

**Risk:** Bitcoin price crashes, mining revenue disappears mid-pivot

**Mitigation:**
- **Seed funding:** Raise $2M-$3M before pivot (18-month runway)
- **Diversified revenue:** Consulting + pilot success fees + mining
- **Burn discipline:** Break-even or profitable at all times
