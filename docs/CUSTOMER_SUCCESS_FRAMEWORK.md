# HYBA PQMC Customer Success Framework

## Overview

This document defines the customer success framework for HYBA's Post-Quantum Mathematical Computing (PQMC) platform, ensuring customers achieve their desired outcomes and maximize value from the platform.

## Customer Success Philosophy

**Mission**: Enable every customer to successfully adopt and derive maximum value from HYBA PQMC through proactive guidance, education, and support.

**Vision**: Become a trusted partner in our customers' scientific and commercial success, driving innovation through post-quantum mathematical computing.

## Customer Journey

### 1. Onboarding (Weeks 1-4)

#### Pre-Onboarding
- **Kickoff Call**: Introduction to customer success team
- **Needs Assessment**: Understand customer goals and use cases
- **Technical Evaluation**: Assess technical requirements and environment
- **Account Setup**: Configure account, API keys, and billing

#### Onboarding Activities
- **Welcome Package**: Documentation, tutorials, and getting started guide
- **Technical Training**: 2-hour training session on platform usage
- **API Integration Support**: Assistance with API integration
- **First Use Case**: Guided implementation of first use case
- **Success Metrics**: Define KPIs and success criteria

#### Onboarding Deliverables
- [ ] Customer success plan document
- [ ] Technical integration guide
- [ ] API credentials and access
- [ ] Training completion certificate
- [ ] First use case deployed

### 2. Adoption (Months 2-6)

#### Adoption Activities
- **Weekly Check-ins**: Progress review and obstacle removal
- **Monthly Business Reviews**: Review usage, value, and goals
- **Best Practices Sharing**: Share industry best practices
- **Feature Updates**: Inform about new features and improvements
- **Community Connection**: Connect with other customers and experts

#### Adoption Metrics
- **Usage Frequency**: Weekly/daily active users
- **Feature Adoption**: Percentage of features used
- **Integration Depth**: Number of integrations
- **Value Realization**: Achievement of defined KPIs

### 3. Growth (Months 7-12)

#### Growth Activities
- **Quarterly Business Reviews**: Strategic review and planning
- **Expansion Opportunities**: Identify additional use cases
- **Advanced Training**: Advanced features and optimization
- **Success Story Development**: Create case studies and testimonials
- **Advocacy Program**: Invite to customer advisory board

#### Growth Metrics
- **Revenue Expansion**: Upsell and cross-sell revenue
- **Usage Growth**: Month-over-month usage increase
- **Customer Satisfaction**: NPS and CSAT scores
- **Referral Generation**: Number of referrals

### 4. Renewal (Months 12+)

#### Renewal Activities
- **Renewal Planning**: 90-day renewal planning process
- **Value Demonstration**: Quantify value delivered
- **Future Roadmap**: Share product roadmap and align with customer needs
- **Renewal Negotiation**: Discuss contract terms and expansion
- **Long-term Partnership**: Discuss strategic partnership opportunities

#### Renewal Metrics
- **Renewal Rate**: Percentage of customers renewing
- **Churn Rate**: Percentage of customers not renewing
- **Expansion Rate**: Percentage of customers expanding
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: Total value over customer lifetime

## Success Metrics

### Customer Health Score

**Components**:
- **Usage Health** (40%): Frequency, depth, and growth of usage
- **Engagement Health** (30%): Response rate, meeting attendance, feedback
- **Sentiment Health** (20%): NPS, CSAT, qualitative feedback
- **Technical Health** (10%): Error rates, support tickets, integration issues

**Scoring**:
- **Green (80-100)**: Healthy, low risk
- **Yellow (60-79)**: At risk, needs attention
- **Red (0-59)**: Critical, immediate action required

### Key Performance Indicators

#### Adoption KPIs
- **Time to First Value**: Days from signup to first successful use
- **Time to Production**: Days from signup to production deployment
- **Feature Adoption Rate**: Percentage of features adopted
- **Weekly Active Users**: Number of active users per week

#### Engagement KPIs
- **Meeting Attendance Rate**: Percentage of scheduled meetings attended
- **Response Rate**: Percentage of communications responded to
- **NPS Score**: Net Promoter Score
- **CSAT Score**: Customer Satisfaction Score

#### Outcome KPIs
- **Goal Achievement Rate**: Percentage of customer goals achieved
- **Value Realization**: Quantified value delivered (time saved, cost reduced, revenue increased)
- **Expansion Revenue**: Revenue from upsell and cross-sell
- **Renewal Rate**: Percentage of customers renewing

## Support Tiers

### Self-Service (Developer)
- **Channels**: Documentation, community forums, email
- **Response Time**: 24 hours
- **Scope**: Technical questions, bug reports, feature requests

### Standard Support (Production)
- **Channels**: Email, chat, phone (business hours)
- **Response Time**: 4 hours (P1), 24 hours (P2-P4)
- **Scope**: Technical issues, best practices, optimization

### Premium Support (Enterprise)
- **Channels**: Email, chat, phone (24/7), dedicated team
- **Response Time**: 15 minutes (P1), 1 hour (P2), 4 hours (P3-P4)
- **Scope**: Strategic guidance, custom integrations, on-site support

## Playbooks

### Onboarding Playbook

#### Week 1: Kickoff
- **Day 1**: Kickoff call, account setup, welcome package
- **Day 2-3**: Technical evaluation, environment setup
- **Day 4-5**: API integration support, first API call

#### Week 2: Training
- **Day 1-2**: Platform training session
- **Day 3-4**: Hands-on workshop
- **Day 5**: Q&A and troubleshooting

#### Week 3: First Use Case
- **Day 1-3**: Guided implementation
- **Day 4-5**: Testing and validation

#### Week 4: Go-Live
- **Day 1**: Production deployment
- **Day 2-3**: Monitoring and optimization
- **Day 4-5**: Success review and planning

### At-Risk Customer Playbook

#### Identification
- **Trigger**: Health score drops below 60
- **Assessment**: Root cause analysis
- **Classification**: Technical, commercial, or strategic

#### Intervention
- **Technical Issues**: Immediate technical support, escalation
- **Commercial Issues**: Account executive involvement, contract review
- **Strategic Issues**: Executive sponsorship, strategic review

#### Recovery
- **Action Plan**: Specific steps to address issues
- **Timeline**: Clear milestones and deadlines
- **Follow-up**: Regular check-ins until recovery

### Expansion Playbook

#### Identification
- **Trigger**: High health score, usage growth, new use cases
- **Assessment**: Expansion opportunities and value potential
- **Proposal**: Tailored expansion proposal

#### Execution
- **Presentation**: Value demonstration and ROI analysis
- **Negotiation**: Contract terms and pricing
- **Implementation**: Expansion deployment and training

## Team Structure

### Customer Success Manager (CSM)
- **Ratio**: 1:50 (Developer), 1:25 (Production), 1:10 (Enterprise)
- **Responsibilities**: Customer relationships, success planning, retention
- **Metrics**: Health score, NPS, renewal rate, expansion revenue

### Technical Account Manager (TAM)
- **Ratio**: 1:100 (Production), 1:25 (Enterprise)
- **Responsibilities**: Technical guidance, optimization, integrations
- **Metrics**: Technical health, adoption rate, time to resolution

### Support Engineer
- **Ratio**: 1:200 (Developer), 1:100 (Production), 1:50 (Enterprise)
- **Responsibilities**: Technical support, troubleshooting, issue resolution
- **Metrics**: Response time, resolution time, customer satisfaction
