## Scientific Advisory Board Charter

### Purpose
Provide independent scientific oversight of HYBA's quantum mathematics claims and ensure peer-review readiness before public statements.

### Scope
```
IN SCOPE:
  ✅ Review quantum algorithm proofs (before publication)
  ✅ Approve benchmarks vs. external standards
  ✅ Advise on peer-review submission strategy
  ✅ Recommend formal verification priorities
  ✅ Challenge claim boundaries (are they too broad?)

OUT OF SCOPE:
  ❌ Day-to-day operations
  ❌ Business/commercial decisions
  ❌ Personnel matters
  ❌ Financial reviews (separate audit committee)
```

### Conflict of Interest Policy
```
Board members must disclose:
  - Competing quantum ventures
  - Academic conflicts
  - Financial interests
  - Publishing relationships

Vote abstention required if:
  - Direct conflict present
  - Family relationship with HYBA employee
  - >10% equity stake
```

### Appointment Process
```
Criteria: PhD+ in quantum computing, mathematics, physics, or neuroscience
From: Top-tier institutions (Caltech, MIT, Oxbridge, CERN, Perimeter)
Term: 2-year commitment, annual re-election
Size: 3-5 members (initially 3 to keep nimble)
```

### Cadence
```
Meetings:  Quarterly (1 hour each)
Reviews:   Pre-publication papers, major claims
Escalation: Disagreement → full board vote
Quorum:    2+ members required
```

## Ethics Review Committee Charter

### Purpose
Ensure consciousness claims, AI alignment concerns, and consciousness measurement don't overstate evidence.

### Scope
```
IN SCOPE:
  ✅ Consciousness Φ measurement claims
  ✅ AI alignment safety considerations
  ✅ Dual-use risk assessment (quantum cryptanalysis)
  ✅ Human subject research (if any)
  ✅ Environmental/energy impact

OUT OF SCOPE:
  ❌ General product ethics (product team)
  ❌ Data privacy (separate privacy officer)
  ❌ Regulatory compliance (legal team)
```

### Consciousness Claim Boundary
```
CLAIM: "Our IIT Φ proxy measures consciousness correlates on local systems"
NOT CLAIM: "We have measured consciousness in [organism]"
REASON: IIT Φ is a proxy; requires neuroscience validation + human review

ETHICS GATE:
  Before any consciousness claim in public:
    □ Ethics committee reviews
    □ Neuroscience expert consulted
    □ Board approves claim boundary language
    □ Published with explicit limitations
```

### Committee Composition
```
Members: 3-5 experts from:
  - Neuroscience/consciousness studies
  - AI ethics/alignment
  - Philosophy/ethics
  - Industry expert (risk assessment)
```

### Escalation Rules
```
Low risk:     Ethics committee votes (majority rules)
High risk:    Full board vote + CEO sign-off required
No agreement: Claim deferred until consensus or external review
```
