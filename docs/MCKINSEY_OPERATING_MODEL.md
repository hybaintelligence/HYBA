## OPERATING MODEL PRINCIPLES

### Principle 1: Evidence-First Operations

**Definition**: Every operation generates cryptographic evidence before execution proceeds.

**Implementation**:
- SHA-256 sealing on all inputs
- Evidence chain extension on all operations
- Cryptographic verification before output release

**Business Impact**: Complete auditability, zero regulatory risk, court-admissible evidence

### Principle 2: Mathematical Sovereignty

**Definition**: All operations are mathematically verifiable and substrate-independent.

**Implementation**:
- φ-fold geometry invariants verified at each operation
- Substrate independence enables sovereign deployment
- Mathematical proofs provide theoretical guarantees

**Business Impact**: Zero vendor lock-in, sovereign deployment, deterministic performance

### Principle 3: Autonomic Self-Healing

**Definition**: The substrate detects and repairs operational drift without human intervention.

**Implementation**:
- Topology-aware drift detection
- Factor-model reconstruction
- Sovereign-gated self-healing approval

**Business Impact**: 80-95% reduction in operational overhead, zero downtime

### Principle 4: Sovereign Governance

**Definition**: All critical operations require three-rail sovereign approval.

**Implementation**:
- Operational approval (real-time)
- Sovereign approval (strategic)
- Regulatory approval (compliance)

**Business Impact**: Complete regulatory compliance, sovereign control, risk mitigation

### Principle 5: Substrate Independence

**Definition**: Intelligence works identically across all hardware substrates.

**Implementation**:
- φ-fold geometry abstracts hardware differences
- Hardware optimization layer adapts to capabilities
- Cross-substrate transfer enables mobility

**Business Impact**: 70-90% cost reduction, supply chain resilience, deployment flexibility
