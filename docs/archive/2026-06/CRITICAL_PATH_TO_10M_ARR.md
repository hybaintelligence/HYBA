**Why:** Nothing happens without sales leadership  
**Timeline:** 1 week (decision + start interviews)

#### Action Item 2: File First Patent ⚠️ CRITICAL
**Why:** Protection before public launch  
**Timeline:** 1 week to file provisional  
**Investment:** $1.5-2K filing fees + attorney time  
**Impact:** IP moat + valuation multiplier

## RISK MITIGATION

### RISK: CRO Can't Be Hired in Week 1
**Contingency:** CEO + VP Product run GTM for 4 weeks while recruiting continues  
**Timeline Impact:** 4-week delay to customer acquisition  
**Mitigation:** Offer equity package to accelerate recruitment

### RISK: Patent Application Rejected
**Contingency:** Work with attorney to revise, refile  
**Timeline Impact:** 2-3 week delay  
**Mitigation:** Provisional patent buys 12 months; attorney should ensure strong application

### RISK: Beta Customers Don't Close
**Contingency:** Adjust messaging, pricing, or product scope  
**Timeline Impact:** 2-4 week delay  
**Mitigation:** CRO interviews non-customers for feedback before Week 2

### RISK: Market Downturn / Recession
**Contingency:** Pivot to cost-saving positioning, extend sales cycles  
**Timeline Impact:** 4-6 week delay to seed close  
**Mitigation:** Prove ROI obsessively; target CFO/Operations (cost centers)

### RISK: Competitor Launches Similar Product
**Contingency:** Accelerate go-to-market, file patents immediately  
**Timeline Impact:** Customer acquisition may slow  
**Mitigation:** Market first (network effects), differentiate on team + credibility
