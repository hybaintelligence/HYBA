# David Medalia Operator Mandate

## Role

David Medalia should be appointed operator-founder of Al Haras, HYBA's sovereign-grade security company.

His mandate is to convert HYBA's existing mathematical and security substrate into a focused, credible, product-led security company serving HYBA, sovereign clients, critical infrastructure, and national-security-adjacent AI deployments.

## Mission

Build Al Haras into the sovereign AI security assurance company for protected execution, critical-infrastructure resilience, classified-environment readiness, and high-assurance defensive AI deployment.

## Strategic mandate

David owns the company-build motion for Al Haras:

1. Convert HYBA capabilities into market-ready security offers.
2. Keep Al Haras security-only and separate from Themis governance/compliance.
3. Build the evidence room required for serious technical diligence.
4. Prepare controlled engagement pathways for sovereign, critical-infrastructure, and integrator clients.
5. Establish legal, export-control, and claim-boundary discipline before external national-security engagement.

## Decision rights

David should have authority over:

- Al Haras company formation plan
- founding team design
- launch offer architecture
- target-account prioritisation
- partner and advisor shortlist
- internal evidence-room requirements
- demo and diligence package requirements
- first 100-day execution cadence

David should not unilaterally approve:

- weapons-adjacent customer commitments
- classified-environment commitments
- export-controlled technical transfers
- public claims beyond the approved claim boundary
- sovereign-client exclusivity
- IP licensing terms from HYBA to Al Haras

Those require HYBA board, legal, export-control, and founder approval.
