# HYBA SLA Template

**Template — legal review required before customer distribution.**

## Uptime commitment

HYBA targets 99.9% monthly uptime for contracted production QaaS, QIaaS, CIaaS, and Quantum Finance API endpoints, excluding scheduled maintenance and customer-caused incidents.

## Maintenance windows

- Standard window: Sundays 02:00–04:00 UTC.
- Emergency maintenance: as needed for security, reliability, or data integrity; HYBA will provide reasonable notice when practicable.

## Support tiers

- Developer: business-hours support; P1 initial response target 1 business day.
- Production: 24x5 support; P1 initial response target 4 hours.
- Sovereign: 24x7 support; P0 initial response target 30 minutes.

## Service credits

Monthly credit formula:

```text
credit = monthly_platform_fee * credit_percentage
```

Example schedule: 99.0%–99.9% uptime = 10%; 95.0%–99.0% = 25%; below 95.0% = 50%. Credits are not cash refunds and must be claimed within the contract notice period.
