## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document provides the complete Department of Defense (DoD) acquisition pathway for HYBA, the first evidence-first agentic intelligence substrate. The pathway aligns with DoD acquisition reform priorities, addresses specific DoD requirements, and provides a clear roadmap from prototype to full deployment across the DoD enterprise.

- Research and development requirement
- Non-traditional vendor engagement

### SBIR/STTR Funding

**Authority**: 15 U.S.C. § 638 (Small Business Innovation Research)

**Topic Areas**:

- **Topic 2**: Cryptographic Evidence Chains for Classified Environments

- **Timeline**: 24 months

**Scalability**:

## DOD-SPECIFIC RISK MITIGATION

### Technical Risks

**Risk**: Mathematical verification complexity
- **Mitigation**: Formal proof validation, continuous verification
- **Contingency**: Manual verification fallback

**Risk**: Cryptographic evidence chain breaks
- **Mitigation**: Redundant evidence chains, continuous monitoring
- **Contingency**: Manual evidence reconstruction

**Risk**: Substrate independence validation
- **Mitigation**: Extensive testing across hardware substrates
- **Contingency**: Hardware-specific optimizations

### Acquisition Risks

**Risk**: Funding instability
- **Mitigation**: Multi-year funding strategy, congressional engagement
- **Contingency**: Phased deployment with incremental funding

**Risk**: Requirements creep
- **Mitigation**: Requirements baseline, change control board
- **Contingency**: Modular architecture for incremental changes

**Risk**: Schedule delays
- **Mitigation**: Agile development, continuous integration
- **Contingency**: Parallel development tracks

### Operational Risks

**Risk**: Operational acceptance
- **Mitigation**: Early operator involvement, training programs
- **Contingency**: Extended transition period

**Risk**: Integration challenges
- **Mitigation**: Early integration testing, API standardization
- **Contingency**: Integration middleware

**Risk**: Security certification delays
- **Mitigation**: Early certification engagement, continuous compliance
