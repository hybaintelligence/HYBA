## KEY REMINDERS

### 1. Send Emails MONDAY MORNING (not afternoon)
- 9 AM is perfect (investors check email early)
- Afternoon sends get lost in inbox clutter
- First thing Monday = highest open rate

### 2. Use the Templates EXACTLY
- Don't overthink customizations
- Don't add your own language (templates are tested)
- Change only: Name, time, your details
- Everything else is proven effective

### 3. Follow the Call Structure PRECISELY
- 0-2 min: Personal (establish rapport)
- 2-5 min: Problem (make it specific to THEM)
- 5-12 min: Solution + proof (show it works)
- 12-25 min: Opportunity + ask (be specific)
- 25-30 min: Close (get commitment)

### 4. Take Notes During Calls
- What resonated with them?
- What questions did they ask?
- What objections did they raise?
- How did they respond to your close?

### 5. Send Thank-Yous SAME DAY
- Within 2 hours of call ending
- Reference specific things they said
- Confirm next steps
- Keep momentum

### If No Response by Tuesday Noon
- Send gentle reminder: "Wanted to check if you got the email..."
- Assume calendar is busy
- Try different time slots if needed

### If They Want to Reschedule
- Offer 2-3 alternative times (don't be rigid)
- Reschedule, don't cancel

- Activate Plan B immediately (don't dwell)
